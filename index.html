Biography

I received the Ph.D. degree in computer science from [Anhui University] of China in 2024. I am currently a postdoctor in [School of Computer Science and Technology] at Anhui University.

His current research interests are mainly graph based visual representation learning, computer vision, and few-shot learning. I have published 8 papers in top journal, such as IJCV, IEEE T-MM, T-CSVT and T-GRS. I am a reviewer for a number of journals, such as the IEEE TRANSACTIONS ON CIRCUITS AND SYSTEMS FOR VIDEO TECHNOLOGY, Parttern Recognition. For more information, see: https://wangxixi950727.github.io/.
